How It Works

From village to courtroom — here's how cases flow through the Justice LK network to registered attorneys.

1

Villager Registers

A Field Officer registers the villager's case, uploads documents, and selects "Private Lawyer" routing. All details captured in Sinhala, Tamil, or English.

2

AI Prepares the File

The system generates an AI case summary and formal court petition. All uploaded documents are packaged into a complete digital case file.

3

Matched to Your Profile

Cases are matched by district and specialisation. You receive the full case file — summary, documents, petition, and client contact — automatically.

4

You Take It From Here

Contact the client, review the file, and proceed to court. Your fee is agreed directly with the client. Justice LK tracks case progress via the token system.
